Welcome to our world of unmatched hospitality and luxurious pleasures. We are a well-known, international chain of luxury hotels that provide first-rate amenities and services in some of the most alluring locations on earth. Singapore, France, the Maldives, Sri Lanka, and the Maldives


  • Luxurious Accommodations - Your utmost comfort and luxury are guaranteed in our tastefully furnished rooms and suites. From the romantic allure of France to the tropical paradise of the Maldives, each room captures the distinctive personality of its setting.
  • Culinary Excellence - Take a culinary adventure with our variety of dining alternatives. Enjoy the best cuisine from around the world and the local area, masterfully prepared by our talented chefs. Each meal is a culinary beauty, from the fresh seafood in Sri Lanka to the exquisite French fare.
  • Spa and Wellness Retreats - At our top-notch spas, rejuvenate your body and soul. Relax with a relaxing massage, splurge on spa services, and take part in holistic wellness programs that are created to improve your health.
  • Event and Conference Facilities - We provide cutting-edge event and conference facilities for both formal and informal gatherings. Every event, from weddings to business meetings, is conducted successfully thanks to the expertise of our team.
  • Adventure and Exploration - Every place presents different chances for exploration and adventure. Explore Galle's extensive history, take a dip in the Maldives' crystal-clear waters, enjoy the rural romance of France, or become immersed in Singapore's dynamic culture.
  • Personalized Concierge - At your disposal and prepared to help with any request is our devoted concierge crew. We make sure your every need is addressed, whether it be by planning trips, coordinating transportation, or accommodating specific requests.

Our Locations

It is our honor to make your stay with us absolutely exceptional at our hotels because we believe that every visitor deserves an outstanding experience. We really anticipate having you stay at one of our magnificent homes. Here, where luxury meets adventure and every moment turns into a treasured memory, your journey starts.

  • Sri Lanka - Our hotel in Galle, Sri Lanka, is tucked away along the picturesque southern coast of the island nation and offers the ideal fusion of traditional grandeur and contemporary luxury. Discover the famous Galle Fort and take in the cultural diversity of this lovely seaside city.
  • The Maldives - On a remote island in the middle of the Indian Ocean, we have a private Maldivian retreat. Overwater bungalows, immaculate beaches, and a world of underwater treasures may all be found here.
  • France - At our charming country chateau, savor the romanticism of France. Our French hotel is a haven of rest and good wine, surrounded by verdant vineyards and ancient villages.
  • Singapore - Our hotel provides an opulent urban getaway in the bustling center of Singapore. Explore the city's colorful culture, go shopping on Orchard Road, or relax in the peace of our contemporary retreat.
