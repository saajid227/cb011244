OUR Destinations

Heritage Retreat-Galle

Sri Lanka

Welcome to a magical retreat hidden in Galle, Sri Lanka's scenic surroundings. Our hotel serves as a starting point for seeing this beautiful, old seaside city's culture and natural beauty. As you enter our universe, you'll notice a tasteful fusion of elegance, tranquility, and historical history. We cordially invite you to experience Galle's wonders from the comfort of our refuge, where the past and present coexist and every second is a priceless gift.

Beach Haven Resort-Mirissa

Sri Lanka

Welcome to a beachfront paradise where the sun kisses the waves and time flows slowly in time with the tides. Our hotel is your entryway to this tropical paradise, which is offered by Mirissa, a gem of Sri Lanka's southern coast, like no other. We cordially invite you to go to our idyllic setting, where the horizon seems to go on forever and the sound of the Indian Ocean's calming song greets and sends you off each day. Here, where memories are built and aspirations are realized, your Mirissa experience begins.

Maldives

Time moves more slowly in this place, slowing to the beat of the waves, and every moment is an invitation to relax and uncover the true meaning of paradise. We cordially invite you to savor the allure of this tropical haven and make your dreams of a holiday to the Maldives a reality. Elysian Parisian Suites

Paris France

Welcome to Elysian Parisian Suites, the center of romance, art, and classic elegance. Singapore
