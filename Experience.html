Spice Route - Indian Cuisine

Spice Route is a colorful and fragrant tour of the various tastes of Indian food. To offer an authentic dining experience, the restaurant is decorated with vibrant colors and vintage furnishings.


Signature Dishes

Tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich tomato sauce are known as "butter chicken." A fragrant rice meal with your choice of lamb, chicken, or veggies is called a biryani. Tandoori Kebabs: A variety of flavorful kebabs that have been marinated and cooked. Grilled cottage cheese with an Indian spice marinade is known as paneer tikka.

Olive Breeze - Mediterranean Cuisine

A taste of the Mediterranean is offered by Olive Breeze, which focuses on using fresh ingredients and providing healthy choices. It has a laid-back vibe that is reminiscent of a Mediterranean beach location.


Signature Dishes

Fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and feta cheese in a lemon-oregano dressing make up a Greek salad. Crispy chickpea patties wrapped on warm pita bread and served with tahini sauce are known as falafel. Baklava a pastry filled with nuts and honey that is formed by layering filo dough

Zen Garden - Asian Cuisine

Zen Garden offers a peaceful environment for savoring the various and savory Asian cuisine meals. Asian aesthetics are used as inspiration for the décor, which creates a calm dining environment.


Signature Dishes

Rice noodles stir-fried with tamarind sauce, peanuts, and either shrimp or tofu. Sushi Rolls a selection of sushi rolls, including spicy tuna and California rolls. Assorted steamed and fried dumplings with dipping sauces on a dim sum platter.

Coastal Flavors - Seafood Restaurant

A seafood lover's dream come true, Coastal Flavors is located by the water. A broad variety of fresh seafood is available, with a focus on seasonal delicacies and local catches.


Signature Dishes

Freshly caught mahi-mahi is cooked to perfection and served with a choice of sauces. Seafood Paella a meal of rice flavored with saffron, mussels, clams, and shrimp. A thick and creamy lobster soup, lobster bisque is the ideal starter for seafood lovers.

Pearl Cafe Beckons

A beautiful cafe, Pearl Cafe Beckons tempts visitors with a delightful selection of coffee, teas, and delectable pastries. The relaxed ambiance is ideal for quick bites or long coffee breaks.


Signature Dishes

Espresso: Various coffee blends and expertly made espresso. Freshly baked croissants, muffins, and danishes are included in the assortment of pastries. Coffee with Flavor: A selection of flavored lattes, cappuccinos, and iced coffees.
